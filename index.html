About Me

I am Nguyen Linh Dan, a Grade 11 Mathematics student at Hanoi-Amsterdam High School for the Gifted, where I’ve pursued academic excellence with a Grade 10 GPA of 9.3 and a Grade 11 GPA of 9.6. My education has nurtured a deep appreciation for analytical thinking, which I channel into my passion for Business Analytics.
My journey extends beyond the classroom, blending data-driven curiosity with creative expression. I find joy in unraveling insights from numbers, aspiring to transform businesses through informed decisions. Simultaneously, my love for theater allows me to tell stories and lead teams, fostering creativity and connection. I dream of studying Business Analytics in the United States or Australia, where I can merge these passions to drive innovation and inspire change.
Guided by a commitment to growth and impact, I strive to contribute meaningfully through research, leadership, and community service, always seeking to balance rigor with artistry.
I have achieved a SAT score of 1450+ and an IELTS score of 6.5+, which reflect my dedication to building a strong academic foundation for future studies abroad.
